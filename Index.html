Run

AWOLNATION

March 17, 2015

Run is the second studio album by American alternative rock band Awolnation, released on March 17, 2015 through Red Bull Records. The album was announced on January 25, 2015 along with a music video of their new song "Hollow Moon".

Band Members

  • Aaron Richard Bruno- Lead Singer
  • Hayden Scott - Drums
  • Zach Irons - Lead Guitar
  • Devin Hoffman - Bass Guitar
